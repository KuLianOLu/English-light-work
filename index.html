Advanced Technology

13 Core Technologies power the AuroraGlow Eye-Care Desk Lamp, setting a new standard in protective lighting solutions. Explore the innovations that make AuroraGlow the smart choice for healthy eyes.

RRT Red Light Technology

Our proprietary RRT (Red Light Recovery Therapy) technology precisely filters harmful blue light in the 380-455nm range while enhancing beneficial red wavelengths. This innovative approach delivers "nutrients" to your eyes, protecting retinal pigments and epithelial cell activity while promoting blood circulation for healthier, more vibrant eyes.

DT Multi-Dimensional Diffusion

Experience visible optical innovation with our DT Multi-Dimensional Diffusion Technology. The system combines a diffused light panel, 5-in-1 corrugated uniform film (PET diffusion film, polyurethane diffusion layer, acrylic resin diffusion film, PET prism film, and back-coated translucent film), and high-density crystal plate with anti-glare structure. Together, they refract and diffuse light to create soft, shadow-free illumination that mimics natural daylight.
